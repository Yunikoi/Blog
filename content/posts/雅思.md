#### 一、因果关系类（Cause & Effect）

| 短语              | 用法                       | 分数 |
| ----------------- | -------------------------- | ---- |
| **account for**   | 导致（后接结果）           | 7.0  |
| **coupled with**  | 加之；连同（连接两个原因） | 7.0  |
| **impetus**       | 推动力（名词，作宾语）     | 7.5  |
| **on account of** | 因为；由于（后接名词）     | 6.5  |

**例句（7分段）**：
> Poor infrastructure, **coupled with** a lack of investment, **accounts for** the slow economic growth.
>
> 基础设施薄弱，加之缺乏投资，导致了经济增长缓慢。

> Government subsidies have provided a strong **impetus** for the development of renewable energy.
>
> 政府补贴为可再生能源的发展提供了强大的推动力。

### 0512

#### 一、核心词&变形（必背）

同义替换（口语换词不重复）
fixation 执念 / craze 狂热 / fascination 强烈着迷 / addiction 沉迷

#### 二、Part 1 高频话题+7分现成答案
适用话题：**Hobbies、Collecting、Celebrities、Social media、Games、Shopping**

##### 1. Do you have any obsession?
Not really, I don’t have any **unhealthy obsession** with anything.
I just have a normal fascination with reading and travelling, nothing extreme.
我没有什么过度痴迷的东西，只是普通喜欢读书和旅行，不算沉迷。

##### 2. What are young people easily obsessed with nowadays?
Lots of youngsters are **obsessed with** social media, short videos and celebrities.
Some even develop an **unhealthy obsession** with online games or luxury brands.
现在很多年轻人沉迷社交媒体、短视频、明星，有的还过度沉迷游戏和奢侈品。

##### 3. Is it good to be obsessed with something?
It depends.
If it’s a positive obsession like learning or sports, it can motivate people.
But if it’s blind obsession, it’s definitely bad for study and life.
分情况，正向痴迷（学习运动）有动力；盲目沉迷就影响生活学习。

##### 4. Why do people have obsessions?
I think it’s mainly because they want entertainment, or they feel empty in daily life.
Also, peer pressure makes many people follow others and become obsessed with trends.
主要为了消遣、生活空虚，还有同龄人压力跟风潮流而沉迷。

#### 四、Part 3 深度抽象话题（7分观点+高分句）
##### 1. Why do people develop an obsession with celebrities?
Many people admire their lifestyle, fame and appearance.
Also, social media amplifies their charm, which fuels people’s obsession.
羡慕明星生活、名气颜值，再加社交媒体放大魅力，助长痴迷。

##### 2. What’s the difference between hobby and obsession?
A hobby is relaxing and controllable, while an obsession is extreme.
People with an obsession can’t stop it, and it may affect their work and life.
爱好可控放松，痴迷极端、无法自控，还会影响工作生活。

##### 3. Should parents stop children’s unhealthy obsessions?
Definitely yes.
Parents should guide kids instead of simply banning things.
They need to help children avoid blind obsession and build right values.
家长要引导而非硬禁止，帮孩子避免盲目沉迷、树立正确价值观。

##### 4. Has modern society created more obsessions for people?
Absolutely.
Social media, online shopping and digital games give people more chances to develop various obsessions than before.
现代社交媒体、网购、游戏让人更容易产生各种沉迷。
